Frequently asked questions.

  • What is CCNA?

    CCNA stands for Certified Cisco Network Associate, the most valued certification in the IT industry. This is a certification offered by the Cisco, USA.

  • Why CCNA?

    Getting a CCNA certification benchmarks your skills as a network engineer against a global collection of best practices, and helps you grow in your career.

  • How much does it cost?

    In addition to the training costs, there's an additional $295 fee payable to Cisco itself to apply for the certification exam.

    That's all.

  • How do I get my CCNA certification?

    In a nutshell, you get trained first, then apply for the certification, and finally take up and clear the exam at a Prometric center.

  • What's your pass guarantee?

    None.

    Providing pass guarantees is unethical, and violates Cisco's code of conduct.

  • How do I register/ make a payment?

    To register, click on the "Register Now" button and fill in the details on the pop-up (you'll need to enable pop-ups on your browser). After submitting, you'll receive payment details by email.

  • How do I get more information?

    Just drop an email to ChikkiEater@mailimate.com with your queries, and we'll revert shortly (usually within hours, never more than a day).
