Frequently asked questions.

  • Which training areas do you cover?

    Our primary training domains are Project Management, Six Sigma, ITIL, and People Management. However, we have provided quality training across Programming, Software Tools, Information Security, and even Financial Modeling. Let us know your requirement, and we’ll get back to you with a proposal.

  • Do you really offer performance guarantees?

    100%. To all clients, for all training we conduct.

  • What are online live classes?

    Online live classes have the instructor deliver lectures online using Cisco WebEx/ GoToMeeting solutions. Attendees will need to log on to the specific link provided to them to participate in the lectures. We use only these two standard solutions.

  • How much will the training cost?

    Direct costs involve only the training fee per candidate/ per training, and would vary by training duration and training subject area. Ball park figures would be in the range of $50 - $120 per hour of training.

     

    Indirect costs involve your team’s time away from work, and situation based costs like travel and accommodation for your team.

  • How do I get more information?

    Just drop us a message using the “Get in touch” link above.
