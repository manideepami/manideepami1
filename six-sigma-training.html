Frequently asked questions.

  • What is Six Sigma?

    Six Sigma is a set of tools and strategies for process improvement originally developed by Motorola. Six Sigma seeks to improve the quality of process outputs by identifying and removing the causes of defects (errors) and minimizing variability in manufacturing and business processes.

  • What is ASQ?

    ASQ or American Society for Quality is a global community of over 80000 quality professionals dedicated to the promotion and advancement of quality tools, principles, and practices. Originally based in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, USA, ASQ currently has members from over 140 countries.

  • How much does it cost?

    In addition to the training costs, there's an additional fee payable to ASQ itself to apply for the certification exam.

    That's all.

  • What is the difference between six sigma and lean six sigma?

    Six Sigma and Lean Six Sigma are an improvement program for an organization. Six Sigma is a set of tools and techniques for process improvement where as Lean six sigma is a process improvement program.

  • Are you an accredited training organization?

    ASQ does not accredit training providers, but we are an IASSC Accredited Training Provider.
