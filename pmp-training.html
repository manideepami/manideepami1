Frequently asked questions.

  • What is PMP?

    PMP stands for Project Management Professional, the #1 certification for project managers globally. This is a certification offered by the Project Management Institute (PMI), USA.

  • Why PMP?

    Getting a PMP certification benchmarks your project management skills against a global collection of best practices, and helps you grow in your project management career.

  • How much does it cost?

    In addition to the training costs, there's an additional $555 fee payable to PMI itself to apply for the certification exam.

    That's all.

  • How do I get my PMP certification?

    In a nutshell, you get trained first, then apply for the certification at www.pmi.org, and finally take up and clear the exam at a Prometric center.

    Detailed description: PMP Handbook

  • What's your pass guarantee?

    None.

    Providing pass guarantees is unethical, and violates PMI's code of conduct.

  • What's a registered education provider?

    A registered education provider (REP for short) is a training prganization whose courses are vetted by PMI and are certified to conform the PMP certification exam requirements.

  • How do I register/ make a payment?

    To register, click on the "Register Now" button and fill in the details on the pop-up (you'll need to enable pop-ups on your browser). After submitting, you'll receive payment details by email.

  • How do I get more information?

    Just drop an email to ChikkiEater@mailimate.com with your queries, and we'll revert shortly (usually within hours, never more than a day).
